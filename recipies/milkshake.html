Milkshake

Description

A milkshake is a sweet beverage made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings or sweeteners such as butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate syrup, or fruit syrup into a thick, sweet, cold mixture.

It may also be made using a base made from non-dairy products, including plant milks such as almond milk, coconut milk, or soy milk. Dry ingredients such as whole fruit, nuts, seeds, candy, or cookies may be incorporated

ingredients

  • banana
  • ice cream
  • milk
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Blend banana, milk, and sugar
  2. Add ice cream and blend again
