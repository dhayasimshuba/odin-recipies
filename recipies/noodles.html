Noodles

Description

Noodles are a popular food enjoyed all around the world in many different forms. They are typically made from wheat, rice, or other grains and can be cooked by boiling or stir-frying. Originating thousands of years ago in Asia, noodles have become a staple in cuisines like Chinese, Japanese, Italian, and Thai.

While instant noodles are a quick and convenient meal, traditional noodles are often handmade and served with fresh vegetables, meats, or sauces. Whether eaten in a spicy broth or with a creamy pasta sauce, noodles are versatile, filling, and loved by people of all ages.
