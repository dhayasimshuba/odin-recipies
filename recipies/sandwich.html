Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is a meal in the form of two pieces of bread with meat, cheese, or some other filling between them. The concept of a sandwich is named after the Fourth Earl of Sandwich, who is credited with inventing it. Sandwiches are a popular type of lunch food, often eaten as part of a packed lunch.

They can be made with a diverse variety of ingredients and are widely sold in various retail outlets. The bread is frequently coated with condiments such as mayonnaise or mustard to enhance its flavor and texture, but may be served plain ("dry").

Ingredients

  • bread
  • veggies
  • cheese
  • mayo

Steps

  1. butter the bread
  2. add sliced veggies
  3. sprinkle salt and pepper
  4. add cheese and mayo
  5. close sandwich and toast
